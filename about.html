Meet Catherine


Catherine is based in Brooklyn, New York. She has loved to bake and beauty products ever since she can remember. In August 2019, Catherine created beauty and baking (B and B). B and B is a lifestyle blog focusing on a healthy and fun approach to baking recipes and beauty products. This blog is all about celebrating good-real, sustainable-food that nourishes your body and clean beauty products that will make you feel your best self. I'm so glad you're here!
